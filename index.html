About Aptove

Aptove is an open source mobile application that enables you to chat with AI agents using the Agent Client Protocol (ACP).

Our mission is to make AI agent interactions accessible, seamless, and powerful on mobile devices. With Aptove, you can connect to various AI agents, have natural conversations, and leverage the power of AI wherever you go.
